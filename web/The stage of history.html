Renowned City of the Three Kingdoms

The Stage of History

As soon as one heard tales of the Three Kingdoms, the desire to visit Jingzhou arises." Mentioned in seventy-two chapters of Romance of the Three Kingdoms, Jingzhou became a household-name the strategically vital prize contested by all. Today, the well- preserved Ancient City Wall standing in the urban center serves as a witness to the history. Repaired and maintained across the Ming and Qing dynasties, the wall stretches 11.28 kilometers and has been acclaimed as "an irreplaceable intact treasure in Southern China."In recent years, the local government has undertaken comprehensive conservation and restoration, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in history and have a communication with the ancients.
