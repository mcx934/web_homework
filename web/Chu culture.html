Chu Culture: A Civilization of Timeless Radiance

Located in the middle reaches of the Yangtze River-blended the Shang and Zhou civilizations, Jingzhou formed a unique material and spiritual cultural system known as Chu Culture.

From bronze metallurgy to lacquer art, from silk embroidery to poetry, a variety of precious artifacts write the prologue to its glorioushistory. From Qu Yuan to Song Yu, a succession of historic figures left spiritual treasures for humanity.

Chu Ci lays the foundation for Chinese romantic literature, while the *Jiu Jiu Shu* demonstrates that China has already established a mature system of mathematical knowledge over two thousand years ago.

Today, with renewed dynamism and growing global influence, Chu Culture continues to showcase the profound charm of Chinese civilization to the world.
